Agenta now has a dark theme. Every page is covered: the playground, registry, evaluations, observability, and the settings screens.

You switch themes from the top bar, next to the version label. There are three options: light, dark, and system. Pick **system** and Agenta follows your operating system, so it turns dark when your machine does and back to light in the morning.

Your choice is saved and stays the same the next time you open Agenta.
