# Go-to-Market OS Process

## Main loop

- understand market and audience
- define positioning and messaging
- define pricing and packaging
- prepare launch and distribution
- measure adoption, conversion, and revenue signals
- review and iterate
- feed residue back into GTM, product, and system knowledge

## Core activities

- market research
- messaging development
- pricing and packaging definition
- content production
- SEO and growth work
- launch planning
- sales enablement
- funnel analysis

## Actors and critics

- design actor = GTM designer
- implementation actor = GTM developer
- verification critic = GTM reviewer
- validation critic = GTM tester

## Likely skills or agents

- market researcher
- product marketer
- pricing and packaging designer
- growth analyst
- SEO specialist
- launch planner
- sales-enablement writer
- DevRel outbound operator

## Main outputs

- positioning
- messaging
- pricing and packaging definitions
- launch assets
- sales assets
- content and SEO outputs
- funnel metrics and growth learnings
