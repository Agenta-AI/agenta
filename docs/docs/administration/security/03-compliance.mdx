# Compliance

Agenta maintains security and compliance standards to protect your data and support enterprise procurement.

## SOC 2 Type II

Agenta is **SOC 2 Type II** certified.

### Requesting the SOC 2 Report

To request access to our SOC 2 Type II report, use our **[Trust Center](https://trustcenter.agenta.ai/)**.

## HIPAA

Agenta is the process of becoming HIPAA certified.

## Trust Center

Our **[Trust Center](https://trustcenter.agenta.ai/)** provides up-to-date information about our compliance status, controls, and policies.

## GDPR

Agenta provides documentation and controls to help business customers meet GDPR-related obligations.

- **Data Processing Agreement (DPA):** https://agenta.ai/docs/administration/security/dpa
- **Sub-processors:** https://agenta.ai/docs/administration/security/sub-processors
- **International transfers:** where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as the **EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)** and the **UK International Data Transfer Addendum**, as described in our DPA.
- **Data regions:** https://agenta.ai/docs/administration/security/data-regions

If you need a countersigned DPA or additional documentation for procurement, contact sales@agenta.ai.

## Enterprise Compliance

For enterprise customers with specific compliance requirements:

### Available Options

- **Dedicated instances** - Enhanced isolation and control
- **Custom data retention** - Configure retention policies
- **Enhanced logging** - Additional audit capabilities
- **Custom security reviews** - Work with your security team

### Compliance Questionnaires

We can assist with:

- Security questionnaires
- Vendor risk assessments
- Due diligence requests

Contact your account representative or sales@agenta.ai for assistance.

## Continuous Compliance

We maintain compliance through ongoing monitoring, regular audits, security reviews, and policy updates.

## Status & Incidents

Monitor our system status and any incidents:

We communicate incidents through:

- Email notifications for affected customers
- Post-incident reports for significant events
