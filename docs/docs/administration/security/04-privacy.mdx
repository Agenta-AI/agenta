# Privacy & Data Handling

Agenta is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## Data We Collect

### Account Data

When you create an account, we collect:

- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Organization name
- Authentication credentials (hashed)

### Usage Data

When you use Agenta, we process:

- **Prompts and configurations** - The prompts you create and manage
- **Evaluation data** - Test sets, evaluation results, and metrics
- **Observability data** - Traces, spans, and logs from your LLM applications
- **Secrets** - API keys you store for LLM providers (encrypted)

### Analytics Data

We collect anonymized analytics to improve our product:

- Feature usage patterns
- Performance metrics
- Error rates

## How We Use Your Data

### To Provide the Service

- Store and manage your prompts
- Run evaluations
- Display observability data
- Authenticate your access

### To Improve the Service

- Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
- Identify and fix bugs
- Develop new features

### We Do NOT

- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your prompts or data to train AI models
- Share your data without your consent (except as required by law)

## Data Protection

### Encryption

All your data is protected by encryption:

| Data State | Encryption |
|------------|------------|
| At rest | AES-256 |
| In transit | TLS 1.2+ |
| Secrets | PGP symmetric encryption |

### Access Controls

- Only authorized personnel can access production systems
- Access is logged and audited
- Principle of least privilege enforced

### Data Isolation

- Your data is isolated from other customers
- Organization-level and project-level isolations
- No cross-tenant data access

## Data Retention

### Active Data

Your data is retained as long as your account is active:

- Prompts and configurations - Retained until deleted
- Evaluation data - Retained until deleted
- Observability traces - Retained per your plan limits

### Deleted Data

When you delete data:

- It is removed from active use in the product
- It may remain in backups for a limited period

### Account Closure

If you close your account:

- Data deletion is initiated upon request
- Timelines depend on your plan and support process

## Your Rights

You have control over your data:

### Access

- View all your data through the UI
- Export your data via API
- Request a data export from support

### Correction

- Update your profile information
- Modify your prompts and configurations
- Correct any inaccurate data

### Deletion

- Delete individual items (prompts, test sets, traces)
- Delete your entire account
- Request complete data erasure

### Portability

- Export prompts and configurations
- Export evaluation results
- Export observability data

## FAQ

### Do you use my data to train AI models?

**No.** Your prompts, evaluation data, and traces are never used to train AI models. Your data is yours.

### Can Agenta employees see my data?

Access to customer data is strictly controlled:

- Only authorized personnel with a business need
- All access is logged and audited
- Used only for support and troubleshooting when needed

### How long do you keep my traces?

Trace retention depends on your plan. Check your plan details for specific retention periods.

### Can I request all my data?

Yes. Contact support to request a complete export of your data.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?

Account cancellation may retain your data for a limited period to allow account recovery. Contact support for deletion requests.
