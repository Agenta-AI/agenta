---
title: "What is the data retention period?"
description: "Data retention by plan"
slug: /faq/platform/data-retention
---

Data retention varies by plan.

| Plan | Retention Period |
|------|------------------|
| Free | 31 days |
| Pro | 91 days |
| Business | 365 days |
| Enterprise | Custom |

After the retention period, traces and associated data are automatically deleted.
