NOTE: bundled Redis is running without a password (the default). This is
fine for local/dev clusters where pod-to-pod traffic is trusted (Redis
is ClusterIP-only), but exposes the cache/queue to any pod in the cluster.
For shared or production clusters, set `redisVolatile.password` and
`redisDurable.password` in your values file, and consider adding a
NetworkPolicy restricting access to the agenta pods.
