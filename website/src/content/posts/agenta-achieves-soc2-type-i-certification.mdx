We're thrilled to announce that Agenta has achieved SOC2 Type I certification, validating our commitment to protecting your LLM development data with enterprise-grade security controls.

## Why SOC 2 Matters for AI Teams

<InlineCTA />

Building production LLM applications involves handling sensitive data at multiple touchpoints—from prompts and datasets to customer observability data. As an open-source platform managing these critical assets, we've always made security our top priority. This certification provides independent verification that our security controls meet the stringent standards enterprise teams demand.

## What's Covered by Our SOC2 Certification

The certification encompasses our entire platform, including:

- Prompt management and versioning system
- Evaluation frameworks and test sets
- Observability tools and data
- Custom workflow deployments

Our security controls cover:

- Data encryption in transit and at rest
- Access control and authentication
- Security monitoring and incident response
- Backup and disaster recovery
- Regular security assessments

## What This Means for Our Users

For teams using Agenta to develop LLM applications, this certification ensures:

- **Robust Security**: Your LLM data is protected by comprehensive policies, processes, and technical safeguards.
- **Enterprise-Readiness**: Our SOC 2 Type I compliance makes Agenta suitable for teams of all sizes—from startups to large enterprises.
- **Third-Party Validation**: Independent auditors regularly review our security controls to ensure ongoing compliance and data protection.

## Next Steps

We remain dedicated to enhancing our security practices. We're already working toward SOC2 Type II certification, which will validate the operational effectiveness of our controls over time.
