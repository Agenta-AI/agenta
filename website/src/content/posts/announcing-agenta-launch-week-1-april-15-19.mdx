## What's Launch Week?

<InlineCTA />

For five consecutive days, we'll be releasing a major new feature each day. We've been working on these updates for months based on your feedback and requests.

While we're keeping the specific details under wraps until each daily announcement, we can tell you that most of these features focus on significantly improving the playground experience for everyone building LLM applications.

## How to Follow Along

- **Daily Blog Posts**: We'll publish a detailed post about each new feature
- **Email Updates**: Short daily emails with the highlights
- **Social Media**: Follow us on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/agenta_ai) and [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/company/agenta-ai) for real-time announcements
- **GitHub**: Star our [repository](https://github.com/agenta-ai/agenta) to see all releases

## Why We're Doing This

Launch Week is our way of showcasing the progress we've made in building Agenta into the tool we all wish existed for working with LLMs. Every feature we're launching directly addresses challenges our users have shared with us.

We can't wait to show you what we've built and hear your thoughts!

Stay tuned for Monday's first announcement, and thank you for being part of our journey.

*The Agenta Team*
