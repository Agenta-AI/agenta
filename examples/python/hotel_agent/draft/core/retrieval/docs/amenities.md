# Amenities at The Agenta Grand Hotel

A summary of on-site facilities, hours, and how to access them. All amenities are open to registered guests; some are also open to the public on a paid basis.

## Pool

We have **two pools**: an indoor heated lap pool on the second floor and an outdoor rooftop pool on the eighteenth floor.

- **Indoor pool**: open daily, **6:00am–10:00pm**. Lap lanes available 6:00am–9:00am. Towels provided poolside.
- **Rooftop pool**: open seasonally, **April through October**, daily **9:00am–9:00pm**, weather permitting. Lounge chairs and shaded cabanas are first-come, first-served. Cabanas can be reserved in advance through the front desk for $50 per day.
- Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. No lifeguard on duty; swim at your own risk.

## Gym (24/7 Fitness Center)

The fitness center is on the third floor and is **open 24 hours** with room-key access. No reservation needed.

Equipment includes treadmills, ellipticals, stationary bikes, two rowing machines, a full free-weight rack (5–80 lb dumbbells), a cable machine, a Smith machine, two benches, and stretching mats. Filtered water and disposable towels are provided. Personal training sessions can be booked through the spa for $90/hour.

## Spa

The spa is on the fourth floor, **open Monday–Sunday, 9:00am–8:00pm**. Booking is recommended; same-day availability is sometimes possible. Reach the spa through the front desk or the in-room phone.

- **Massage**: Swedish, deep tissue, hot stone. 60 minutes / 90 minutes — $140 / $200.
- **Facials**: classic and deep-cleansing. 60 minutes — $130.
- **Body treatments**: salt scrub, mud wrap. 75 minutes — $170.
- **Couples rooms** are available on request.

The spa includes a steam room and a dry sauna, complimentary for guests with any treatment booking and $25 day-pass otherwise.

## Restaurant — The Atrium

A full-service restaurant on the lobby level. Cuisine is modern American with seasonal Mediterranean influences. Reservations recommended for dinner; not required.

- **Breakfast**: daily, **6:30am–10:30am**. Continental and a full hot menu. Around $24 per person, or included on certain rate packages.
- **Lunch**: daily, **11:30am–2:30pm**. Sandwiches, salads, small plates. Average entrée $18–$26.
- **Dinner**: daily, **5:30pm–10:00pm**. Three-course tasting menu available. Average entrée $28–$42. Wine list curated locally.
- **Room service**: available daily, **6:30am–11:00pm**. Menu in-room or via the agent.

## Bar — The Atrium Lounge

Adjacent to the restaurant. Cocktail-focused with a small wine and beer list. Light bites available.

- **Open**: daily, **4:00pm–1:00am**. Last call at 12:30am.
- Live jazz Thursday through Saturday, 8:00pm–11:00pm.

## Business Center

On the lobby level, **open 24 hours** with room-key access. Two workstations with internet, a color printer, scanner, and a small private call booth. Stationery and basic supplies on request from the front desk.

## Conference and Meeting Rooms

Three meeting rooms (capacities 8, 16, and 40). Available for booking through the front desk or the agent. Standard rates start at $150/hour for the smallest room, $400/hour for the boardroom (capacity 16), and $800/half-day for the larger room. AV setup, catering, and whiteboards are available add-ons.

## Other on-site services

- **Concierge desk**: lobby, daily 7:00am–11:00pm.
- **Valet parking**: $45/night. Self-parking $30/night in the attached garage.
- **Laundry and dry cleaning**: same-day return for items submitted before 9:00am, Monday through Saturday.
- **Bike rentals**: $15/day, includes helmet and lock. Available at the concierge desk seasonally.
