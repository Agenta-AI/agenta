# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common guest questions at The Agenta Grand Hotel. For full policy detail, see the policy documents (cancellation, modifications, upgrades, pets, fees, escalation).

## Check-in, checkout, and arrival

**What time is check-in and checkout?**
Check-in is **3:00pm local time**. Checkout is **11:00am local time**.

**Can I check in early?**
Early check-in is offered when a room is ready before 3:00pm, on a best-effort basis. There is no fee. We cannot guarantee early check-in, but we can hold luggage at the front desk if your room isn't ready yet.

**Can I get late checkout?**
Yes. Late checkout up to **1:00pm is free** for all guests. After 1:00pm, the rate is $25 per hour, up to a 4:00pm maximum. **Gold and Platinum** guests get free late checkout up to **2:00pm**, with the same 4:00pm maximum.

**How do I get to the hotel from the airport?**
Pellam International Airport is about 35 minutes by airport-express subway from Linden Avenue Station, two blocks from the hotel. A taxi or rideshare runs roughly 25 minutes outside rush hour. The concierge can arrange a private car for around $75 one-way.

## Booking and pricing

**Is breakfast included?**
Breakfast is **not** included on standard rates. Some packages and promotional rates include breakfast — your booking confirmation will say so explicitly. Otherwise, breakfast at The Atrium is around $24 per person.

**Why is my total higher than the room rate I saw?**
Because the all-in total includes the **14% occupancy tax** (on the room rate), the **$35-per-night resort fee** (waived for Platinum), and any pet fees or add-ons. The agent always quotes the all-in total before booking. See the fees document for a worked example.

**Can I cancel for free?**
That depends on your rate type and tier. **Flexible** rates get a full refund up to the cutoff. **Advance** rates get 50% as future-stay credit (no cash). **Non-refundable** rates do not refund. The cutoff is **24 hours before check-in** for Standard guests and **6 hours** for Gold and Platinum. See the cancellation document for full detail.

**Can I change my booking after I've made it?**
Yes, up to two times per booking. Changes are **free at 48+ hours before check-in**, and a **$25 modification fee** applies inside that window (waived for Gold and Platinum). Some rate types have stricter rules — Advance rates can shift dates but cannot change room class.

## Logistics

**Is there wifi? Is it free?**
Yes. Wifi is included in the resort fee for all guests. The network is `AgentaGrand-Guest`; the password is on the welcome card in your room.

**Is there parking?**
Yes. **Valet parking** is $45 per night. **Self-parking** in the attached garage is $30 per night. Both are added to your room folio.

**What's the smoking policy?**
The hotel is **fully non-smoking**, including all rooms, balconies, and indoor public spaces. A $250 cleaning fee applies if smoking is detected in a guest room. The rooftop pool deck has a designated outdoor area for smoking when the pool is open.

## Pets and animals

**Can I bring my dog?**
Yes, in pet-friendly rooms, with a **$100-per-stay pet fee**. Limits: up to **2 pets per booking**, **50 lbs maximum** per pet. Pet-friendly rooms are a subset of inventory and may be sold out on busy dates — the agent will check separately.

**My dog is over 50 lbs. Can you make an exception?**
Unfortunately not — the 50-lb limit is firm. We can refer you to **Bayside Pet Boarding**, our kennel partner, and the front desk arranges the referral.

**I'm bringing a service animal. What do I need to provide?**
Nothing. Service animals are always permitted, with no fee, no weight check, and no documentation required. Just let us know at booking so we can prepare the room.

## Accessibility

**Do you have accessible rooms?**
Yes. We have ADA-compliant rooms with roll-in showers, grab bars, lower fixtures, and visual fire-alarm signaling. Mention accessibility needs when booking and we'll match the right room. The lobby, restaurant, gym, spa, and pools are all step-free.

**Are service animals welcome in all areas?**
Yes — including the restaurant, lobby, and pool deck.

## Kids and family

**Are kids welcome?**
Absolutely. Children under 12 stay free in the same room when using existing bedding. Cribs and rollaway beds are available on request. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult at the pools.

**Is there a kids' menu?**
Yes, at The Atrium for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Items run $8–$14.

## Groups, events, and special bookings

**I'm planning a group booking.**
Group bookings (typically 5+ rooms or any tied event) are handled by our group-sales team rather than the agent. The agent can pass you to a human for that — please share approximate dates and group size.

**Can I rent a meeting room?**
Yes. Three sizes are available (capacities 8, 16, and 40). Rates start at $150/hour. AV and catering can be arranged. Bookings go through the front desk or the agent.

## Other

**Can I leave luggage after checkout?**
Yes, free of charge. Bags are tagged at the front desk and held until you pick them up later that day.

**Do you have a fridge or microwave in the room?**
All rooms have a mini-fridge. Microwaves are not standard but are available on request, subject to availability.

**What if I'm not happy with my stay?**
Tell us. The agent can dispatch a service ticket for minor issues immediately. For complaints involving staff or property concerns, the agent will escalate to a human team member who can review and respond.
