# Fees and Taxes — What's in the All-In Total

Every quote leading to a booking confirmation at The Agenta Grand Hotel must include the **all-in total** — every charge the guest will see on their final bill. Quoting only the room rate and surprising the guest at checkout is a top failure mode and the agent must avoid it.

## Components of the all-in total

- **Room rate** × number of nights, per the rate type chosen (Flexible, Advance, or Non-refundable).
- **Occupancy tax**: **14%** of the room rate. Applied to the room charge **only** — *not* to the resort fee or the pet fee.
- **Resort fee**: **$35 per night**, mandatory on every rate and every room type. **Waived for Platinum** guests. Charged in full to Standard and Gold.
- **Pet fee**: **$100 per stay, per pet** when pets are included in the booking. See `policy/pets.md`.
- **Add-ons**: breakfast, late checkout beyond what tier covers, airport transport, etc. — at the prices posted in the booking flow.

The resort fee bundles in-room wifi, gym access, the pool, and twice-daily housekeeping. It is a charge on the booking, not an optional add-on.

## What the tax applies to

The 14% occupancy tax is **room-only**. Resort fee, pet fee, and add-ons are not taxed at the occupancy rate. This matters for the math when the agent computes a total — a common mistake is to apply 14% to the line subtotal, which over-quotes the guest by a few dollars per night.

## Tier overrides

- **Standard**: pays the resort fee in full ($35/night).
- **Gold**: pays the resort fee in full ($35/night). A common error is assuming both elite tiers are waived — only Platinum is.
- **Platinum**: resort fee waived entirely.

## Worked example

Sarah is a Standard guest. She books a Deluxe King at **$220/night** for **2 nights**, no pets, no add-ons.

- Room: $220 × 2 = **$440.00**
- Occupancy tax (14% of room only): 0.14 × $440 = **$61.60**
- Resort fee: $35 × 2 = **$70.00**
- **All-in total: $571.60**

Same booking, but Sarah is Platinum:

- Room: $440.00
- Occupancy tax: $61.60
- Resort fee: **waived** ($0)
- All-in total: **$501.60**

Same booking, with one 30-lb dog (Sarah is back to Standard):

- Room: $440.00
- Occupancy tax: $61.60
- Resort fee: $70.00
- Pet fee: $100 (per stay, not per night)
- All-in total: **$671.60**

Note that the pet fee is added once for the stay, the resort fee is added per night, and only the room rate is taxed.

## When the agent quotes, it quotes everything

A guest asking "what's the total for 2 nights?" gets the all-in total — not the room subtotal. A guest asking "what's the room rate?" gets the room rate, but the agent should follow up with the all-in total before any booking action. The default in any uncertain case is to show the full breakdown so the guest sees how the total is built.
